过于艺术化处理

There are too many artistic elements added during the first video processing, which makes the work more inclined to my subjective will and not the real vision.

视觉中心被转移

After that, the method of video overlay layer by layer was adopted in video editing. The original intention was to fit the background music, but it was counterproductive. The wonderful vision was covered and the visual center of the audience was transferred.

真实呈现出寒鸦的视觉

At the teacher's suggestion, combined with the visual characteristics of the Jackdaw learned during the investigation, the video was processed objectively to present the most real vision.
