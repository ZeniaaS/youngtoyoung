Animal Visual Illustrated Handbook-Jackdaw

门：脊索动物门  纲：鸟纲
目：雀形目          科：鸦科
属：鸦属              种：寒鸦

据《辞海》解释，寒鸦，亦称"慈鸟"，"小山老鸹"。鸟纲，鸦科。体长可达35厘米，上体除颈后羽毛呈灰白色外，其余部分黑色，胸腹部灰白色。在我国大多终年留居北部，冬季亦见于华南。寒鸦早在《诗经小雅小弁》就出现了:"弁彼鸒斯，回飞提提。形似乌鸦，小如鸽，腹下白，喜群飞，鸣声"呀呀",又名雅乌。这就是寒鸦了。
According to "Cihai", Jackdaw, also known as "Love Bird","Hill Old Man". Birds, Crows. The body length can reach 35cm, and the upper body is gray except the feathers behind the neck, the rest are black, and the chest and abdomen are gray.In China most of the year-round remain in the northern winter also observed in South China. As early as the Book of Songs, Xiaoya and Xiaoyan, the Jackdaw appeared: "Come on, come back to fly to titi. "Shaped like a crow, small as a pigeon, white under the belly, happy group flying, singing "Yayah", also known as ya Wu. This is the Jackdaw.
